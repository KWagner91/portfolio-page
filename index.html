About Me

I am a self-taught Front-End Web developer with Python experience based in France. I provide responsive front-end development as well as functional programming.

In my past experience as a coder I have acquired an eye for detail and functionality of my products. What I love most about my work is being able to bring my vision and ideas closer to a world-wide audience.

I would describe myself as a cosmopolitan, who has been living most of adult life in Switzerland, France and UK. My experiences abroad not only strengthened my organization skills but also allowed me to interact with people of different cultures and backgrounds.

I have a Master of Science degree from University Vienna, after 2 years in biological research I found my live-long passion for coding. Apart from my Tech interest, I love literature, wool-work and bunnies 🐰.
